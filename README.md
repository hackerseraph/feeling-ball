# Feeling Ball

In loving memory of Dr. Rita Goss.

A digital version of the therapeutic feeling ball game, built to help kids and adults recognize and express emotions anywhere, anytime.

## What is a Feeling Ball?

The feeling ball (also known as a Thumball) is a hands-on therapeutic tool used in counseling, classrooms, and group therapy settings. It's a soft, throwable ball covered with emotion words and prompts. When someone catches it, they read the word under their thumb and share a time they felt that way.

The physical version has been used for years by therapists, teachers, and counselors to help children:
- Build emotional vocabulary beyond "good" or "bad"
- Practice identifying and naming specific feelings
- Learn that emotions are normal and everyone experiences them
- Develop emotional intelligence through repeated reflection

## Why a Digital Version?

You can't always have a physical ball with you. Therapy sessions end, school counselors have limited supplies, and families don't always have access to specialized tools. This app solves that problem.

With this digital feeling ball, you can:
- Practice emotional awareness during car rides, waiting rooms, or at home
- Use it as a daily check-in tool without needing physical materials
- Make emotional reflection accessible on any device with a browser
- Maintain continuity between therapy sessions

## How It Works

Roll the ball by clicking the button or dragging it with your mouse. When it stops, you'll see an emotion word and a prompt: "I feel [EMOTION] when..."

The game follows the same principle as the physical version: the randomness takes the pressure off choosing what to share, and the open-ended prompt encourages personal reflection without forcing a specific answer.

## Features

- 24 different emotion words covering a range of feelings
- Smooth 3D rotating ball with a retro arcade aesthetic
- Drag-to-spin interaction for a tactile feel
- Typewriter text effect with blinking cursor
- Works on desktop and mobile devices

## Technical Details

Built with vanilla JavaScript and HTML5 Canvas. The ball uses a truncated icosahedron geometry (soccer ball structure) with pentagons and hexagons, rendered with depth-sorted wireframe edges and face culling for a clean 3D effect.

## Running Locally

Just open `index.html` in any modern web browser. No build process, no dependencies, no installation required.

## Background

This project was inspired by the therapeutic value of physical feeling balls and the need to make emotional learning tools more accessible. The original Thumball concept has been helping people process emotions since the early 2000s, particularly in educational and therapeutic settings where concrete, playful tools help break down barriers to emotional expression.
